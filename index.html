BMW5

BMW M5 is capable of accelerating from 0 to 60 mph (0 to 97 km/h) in around 3.1 to 3.4 seconds. Its top speed is electronically limited to 155 mph (250 km/h) in most versions, but with optional packages, it can reach higher speeds, typically up to around 190 mph (306 km/h) or more.
